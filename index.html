A little about our City

Clearwater, Florida, nestled on the Gulf Coast, is renowned for its stunning white-sand beaches, vibrant culture, and welcoming atmosphere. With its idyllic climate and crystal-clear waters, Clearwater is a haven for beach enthusiasts and outdoor adventurers. Clearwater Beach, often ranked among the best beaches in the United States, offers powdery sand and breathtaking sunsets, making it a magnet for tourists and locals alike. Beyond its shores, the city hosts a variety of attractions, including the Clearwater Marine Aquarium, known for its resident dolphins and the famous Winter the dolphin from the "Dolphin Tale" movies. The area boasts a lively downtown scene, with diverse dining options, shops, and entertainment venues. Additionally, Clearwater is a hub for cultural events, from music festivals to art exhibitions. The city's relaxed ambiance, combined with its natural beauty and array of activities, renders Clearwater a delightful destination for visitors seeking a perfect blend of coastal charm and recreational opportunities.
